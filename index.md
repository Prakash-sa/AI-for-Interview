## üí° Tips for Interview Success

1. **Understand the concepts** - Don't just memorize, grasp the fundamentals
2. **Practice explaining** - Be able to explain concepts in simple terms
3. **Code along** - Actually implement the concepts shown
4. **Real-world examples** - Connect concepts to practical applications
5. **Ask clarifying questions** - In interviews, clarify ambiguous questions
6. **Think out loud** - Show your reasoning process
7. **Keep learning** - AI evolves rapidly, stay updated
