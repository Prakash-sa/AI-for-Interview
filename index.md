<div class="home-section">
  <div class="tips-box">
    <h2 class="home-section-heading">How to Use These Guides</h2>
    <div class="tips-grid">
      <div class="tip-item"><span class="tip-num">1</span><span class="tip-text"><strong>Read Q&amp;As in order</strong> — they build on each other progressively.</span></div>
      <div class="tip-item"><span class="tip-num">2</span><span class="tip-text"><strong>Explain without looking</strong> — practice out loud to test real retention.</span></div>
      <div class="tip-item"><span class="tip-num">3</span><span class="tip-text"><strong>Code along</strong> — implement examples shown to solidify understanding.</span></div>
      <div class="tip-item"><span class="tip-num">4</span><span class="tip-text"><strong>Connect to real projects</strong> — anchor each concept to a practical use case.</span></div>
      <div class="tip-item"><span class="tip-num">5</span><span class="tip-text"><strong>Think out loud</strong> — in interviews, narrate your reasoning step by step.</span></div>
      <div class="tip-item"><span class="tip-num">6</span><span class="tip-text"><strong>Clarify before answering</strong> — ask the interviewer to narrow ambiguous questions.</span></div>
